Why listen music?

Music has a powerful effect on emotions. It can uplift spirits, provide comfort during challenging times, and help individuals process and express their feelings. Whether it's listening to joyful music to boost mood or finding solace in sad songs during times of sadness, music can help individuals regulate and navigate their emotions.Listening to music can be an effective way to reduce stress and promote relaxation.
